Privacy Policy

At Lost & Found Platform, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you use our app. This includes:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data (if you enable location services)
  • Item descriptions and photos
  • Communication between users (messages, chat logs)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate the matching of lost and found items
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Send you notifications and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Other users (to facilitate item returns)
  • Service providers (for app maintenance and support)
  • Law enforcement (if required by law)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccuracies in your information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of certain communications

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at info@lostandfound.com.
